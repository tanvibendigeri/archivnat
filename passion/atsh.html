Agar Tum Saath Ho - Alka Yagnik, Arijit Singh

Agar Tum Saath Ho is a stunning duet between a man and woman whose relationship is on the edge of breaking. It perfectly captures the duality of their situation. She is saying that everything in her life is better when she's around him. However, he doesn't see a point to their relationship because she believes he's someone he's not and that his life is painful either way. It's her desperate attempt at getting him to stay and his final plea to get her to let him go. It's beautiful storytelling that is full of intense emotion.
